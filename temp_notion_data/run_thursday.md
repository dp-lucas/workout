# THUR: Intervals

## Warm Up

**Instructions:** Complete once in a circuit:

- **Ankle Circles** — 10 each side
- **Bodyweight Standing Single Leg Calf Raises** — 10 each side (slow and controlled)
- **Elephant Walk** — 10 each side
- **Leg Swings - Front to Back** — 10 each side
- **Leg Swings - Side to Side** — 10 each side

## Workout: Intervals

**Warm up:**
Run for 15 minutes at an easy pace (120-150 heart rate), include a 30 second fast effort at minutes 8, 10 & 12.

Walk rest for 2 minutes, then…

**Intervals:**
10x 400m at your target 5km pace, with 100m walk rest between intervals

**Cool down:**
Run for 5-10 minutes at an easy pace (120-150 heart rate)
